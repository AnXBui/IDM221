Founded Found Find

Wayfinding | Editoral

The Challenge

This project aims to create a pocket guide / map of the Historic District of Philadelphia. The guide must not only help navigate the many historic oddities and landmarks of Philadelphia, but also embodies the spirit of the neighborhood in a visual way. "Founded Found Find" is the end result of this.

The Goals

  • Found History

    The brochure must create a sense of discovery and "found" history" – history experienced in a new and interactive way.

  • Urban Collage

    The brochure should combine the mixing of history and urbant atmosphere that is emblematic of Philadelphia as a city.

  • Map & Guide

    The brochure will acts as both a map and guide at the sametime, while being pocket-sized.

The Journey

The brochure utilize a old script font as its basis. And as the brochure unfold, it reveals a captivating collage of history tibits, found urban imageries, mixing with bold experiemental layout accentuated by pop of bold color. This unfolding should also tell a journey of adventure and discovery.

The Conclusion

Overall, "Founded Found Find" is an experiemental, interactive, and dynamic experience that blend the historic oddities and the urban landscape of Philadelphia, all contained within a pocket-sized map-guide that promotes exploration and discovery.
