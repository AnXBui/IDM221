Awbury: Nature for All

Branding | Case Study

The Challenge

Awbury is a non-profit organization that manages the Awbury Arboretum – a green space open to the public situated in the center of German Town, Philadelphia. This project aims to imagine a new visual system to revitalize the brand image of the arboretum as a place where nature and community takes root.

The Goals

  • Community at heart

    The new system must put the message of community at the center, and evoke a spirit of collaboration and welcoming nature of the property as a place for everyone.

  • Adaptable and Engaging

    Awbury's visual system must be able to adapt to a variety of phyiscal and digital medium, while remaining a distinctly engaging cohesive langauge that must also engage with audience of all ages.

  • Nature for All

    The visual language must evoke an image of wild and organic nature that exists in the center of an urbant environment: a green shelter admist the busy urban life.

The Journey

Awbury Arboretum's visual system starts with the logo. Using two abstracted of two "A"s, the logo is created as an abstract mark that is letterform-like (suggesting a sense of community) but also rustic and organic. The typeface Baskerville was chosen for its similar weight to the mark and for its nod to Awbury's history.

The typographic system borrows different typefaces both modern and classic to showcase the sense of unity and community that is rooted in Awbury's history. The serif heading is meant to evoke timelessness while the sans serif body text is utilitarian in function while modern in form.

For visual language I was looking something that have both the spirit of community and a feeling of nature. For inspiration I took the abstracted forms of the stone bricks found at the Francis Cope House lying at the heart of Awbury. Simplifying their forms and adding a touch of wildness and texture, I created composition that evoke a sense of unity as well as nature, all while having a positive and energetic spirit.

The Conclusion

Awbury new brand utilizes natural elements inspired from materials found on-site to create a unique brand experience that not only encompass many different medium, but also create a sense of community and collaboration in a space of greenery and nature.
