Sacred Forms, Virbant Soul

Book Design | Editoral

The Challenge

This project challenge is to create a robust artist catalogue for Moe Brooker, – a Philadelphia-based artist with a vision for vibrant abstract paintings that evoke a sense of religious connection. The catalogue must express the content within, and convey the meaning and spirit of Moe Brooker's collection of works.

The Goals

  • Uncoventionally Formal

    The catalogue must express Brooker's strong connection to the idea of divine inspiration, worship, and light as a medium of the divine and the spiritual.

  • Playfully Colorful

    The catalogue must express the vibrancy and freeform boldness of Brooker's use of colors, patterns, and various lineworks, which must also express his evolving style.

  • Creatively Inspired

    The catalogue must borrow and inspire the elements from Brooker's work but they should be in a meaningful and insightful way.

The Journey

The book aethestics is mostly founded on the idea of light, as the chapters can be identified by their unique light gradient emitting from the center of the book. And each chapter of the book follows a certain themes involving lines, and shape that evolves over the catalogue – which reference either the text or the elements of Brook's signature style.

The Conclusion

The catalogue is strong and interesting visually with many references to both the themes and forms of Brooker's artworks, while also exploring many interesting typographic layouts and formal geometric compositions.
